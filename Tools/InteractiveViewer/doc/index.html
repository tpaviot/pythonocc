View Manipulation

  1. Left Click = Select
  2. Middle Click = Re-centre screen
  3. Middle-Mouse Move = Rotate
  4. Shift & Right-Mouse Move = Rotate
  5. Right-Mouse Move = Pan
  6. Middle & Right-Mouse Move = Zoom
  7. CTRL & Right-Mouse Move = Zoom
  8. CTRL & SHIFT & Right-Mouse Move = Create a zoom window
